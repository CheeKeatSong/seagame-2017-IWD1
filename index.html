Volunteer Program

WAU FACTOR

"Wau Factor" represents a combination of the fundamental factors and traits such as Positivity, Dedication, Perseverance and Courage that make Kuala Lumpur 2017 volunteers special. Through this programme, it will be able to attract people from all walks of life to positively contribute to the volunteer network and ensure the smooth running of the games as they showcase their own talent and abilities.
